Thoughts on Grace

my grace is sufficient for the meek... Ether 12:26

In the Strength of the Lord

Brothers and sisters, please pay particular attention to the word grace as it is used in the verse I just read. In the Bible Dictionary we learn that the word grace frequently is used in the scriptures to connote a strengthening or enabling power: "The main idea of the word is divine means of help or strength, given through the bounteous mercy and love of Jesus Christ."

Here I am!
